Bio

Award-winning professional DJ living in Accra, Ghana.

Samuel Opoku Adom, popularly known as AD DJ, is a Ghanaian Disc Jockey, Music Artiste and a Creative Curator. AD DJ shot to fame in 2014 when he joined Ghana’s leading urban music and entertainment station YFM as a Radio DJ. He plays on Accra's biggest on-air youth radio programme, YCampus Express show.

AD DJ is also a Resident DJ at Front/Back Accra, Ghana’s top members-only club which is a favourite hangout hub for big global superstars such as Kendrick Lamar and Chance The Rapper.

He performed a 45-minutes set at one of Africa’s biggest cultural festivals, Afrochella in 2021, headlined by Wizkid. He was again billed to perform in 2022, headlined by Burna Boy

“He has played in some of the best nightclubs in Ghana namely; Twist Nightclub, Luna Bar and more, during peak periods in Summer and December.

AD DJ also plays at Amapiano and Brunch which is one of Ghana’s biggest day parties, witnessing performances from top artistes in Africa and beyond. AD DJ is a multi-award winning DJ and was nominated at the last edition of the Ghana Entertainment Awards, USA for Best DJ Act. His craft transcends DJ’ing. He is an artiste and an occasional playlist curator.
